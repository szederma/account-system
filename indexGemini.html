Redefining Musical Interaction

Distanzuino is more than just an instrument; it's an experience. Born from a passion for music and technology, it invites you to play, learn, and create in a completely new way.

Imagine conducting an orchestra with the wave of your hand, or composing melodies by simply moving through space. Distanzuino makes this a reality. Using high-precision laser sensors, it detects your hand movements, translating them into rich, expressive musical tones.

It's designed to be intuitive for beginners yet powerful for experienced musicians. Beyond performance, Distanzuino is an educational tool, offering a hands-on approach to understanding programming, sensor technology, and the fundamentals of music.

Our mission is to inspire creativity and make the world of music and technology accessible and exciting for everyone.

Core Capabilities

Distanzuino is packed with features designed for intuitive play, learning, and customization.

Touchless Play

Play music without physical contact. Move your hands over sensors to control tones intuitively and precisely.

Smart Web Interface

Customize sounds, choose instruments, adjust volume, and visualize tones in real-time via a user-friendly web app.

Bluetooth Audio

Connect wirelessly to Bluetooth speakers or headphones for rich, high-quality sound output.

Educational & Customizable

Designed to teach programming and music. Modify tones, code, and features with basic coding skills.

Real Instrument Tones

Emulates instruments like flute, piano, and cello using harmonic analysis and dynamic tone shaping.

3D-Printed Modular Design

Compact, foldable, and sleek. Magnetically connected parts allow easy transport and scalability.

High-Precision Sensors

VL53L1X laser sensors detect hand distance with extreme accuracy, immune to environmental interferences.

Future-Proof Innovation

Constantly evolving with new versions planned: mini models, screen-based layouts, and enhanced sound realism.

The Magic Behind the Music

Distanzuino combines cutting-edge hardware with intelligent software to create a seamless musical experience.

1

Sense Motion

High-precision laser sensors (VL53L1X) accurately measure the distance and position of your hands in real-time.

2

Process & Translate

An onboard microcontroller processes this data, translating your movements into musical notes and control signals.

3

Create Sound

The generated music is outputted via Bluetooth or managed through the intuitive web interface for customization.
