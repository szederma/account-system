Meet DistanzuinoBT

DistanzuinoBT is a smart, touchless musical instrument you play with gestures. Using laser sensors and Bluetooth, it turns the distance between your hands and the device into real, customizable sounds. Controlled through an interactive website, it’s fun, portable, and perfect for exploring music in a whole new way.

And why is it your instrument?
Distanzuino isn’t just another musical instrument — it’s your instrument.
With the help of our website, you can learn how music works and how sound is created. Not only can you explore the inner workings of the tones you hear, but you can also be the one to shape them. Here, you’re free to discover, learn, and build the musical instrument you want to create.

How It Works

From a simple gesture to a beautiful note, here’s a look at the technology inside Distanzuino.

1. Gesture Detection

You place your hand over a note area on either side of the device. The laser sensors measure the exact distance from your hand in real time.

2. Bluetooth Transmission

An onboard microcontroller instantly reads the distance values and sends them via Bluetooth to your phone, tablet, or PC.

3. Web App Interpretation

The Distanzuino web application receives the distances and converts them into tones based on pre-defined tone maps and instrument profiles.

4. Realistic Sound Generation

The web app plays tones using measured harmonics and attack-phase timing to match real instruments like the piano, flute, or cello. You hear the result through your device or other connected speakers.

5. Customization

You can adjust tones, volume, and instruments right in the browser. The app also shows which notes are playing real-time.
